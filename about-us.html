Read More About Us

Welcome to Al-Imamu Shafi'i International Academy, a premier primary and secondary school dedicated to providing an exceptional education experience to students from around the world. We are located in SABON UNGUWAN DAN IYA MAKAMA NEW EXTENSION, BAUCHI BAUCHI STATE and have been serving the community since 2007-2023
Our school is named after Al-Imamu Shafi'i, a prominent Islamic scholar who emphasized the importance of knowledge and education in Islam. At Shafi'i International Academy, we strive to integrate Islamic values and principles into our curriculum, and to create a learning environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking
Our experienced and dedicated teachers are committed to providing the highest quality education to our students. They use a variety of teaching methods and strategies to engage students in the learning process, and to help them develop the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed in their academic and personal lives.
Thank you for considering Al-Imamu Shafi'i International Academy for your child's education. We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our programs, facilities, and community.

Best Strategy

At Al-Imamu Shafi'i International Academy, our strategy is centered around providing a holistic, high-quality education to our students that prepares them for success in all aspects of life.

Creative Ideas

STEAM Education: We integrate Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) into our curriculum, encouraging students to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. Our students engage in hands-on projects, experiments, and artistic endeavors, fostering their curiosity and enabling them to apply their knowledge in practical ways.
